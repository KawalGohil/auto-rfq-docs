**Target:** CNC/VMC Shop Owners & Estimating Engineers in Gujarat

## The "Show, Don't Tell" Strategy
*Never walk in and just talk about AI. Walk in with an iPad or a laptop and ask them to test it live.*

### 1. The Hook (Under 30 seconds)
"Hi [Name], I know you're busy so I'll be quick. My team and I are local engineering students, and we've built a software tool specifically for CNC and VMC shops. We noticed estimators spend hours manually typing out dimensions, tolerances, and material grades from complex RFQ blueprints into Excel. We built a tool that does it instantly."

### 2. The Demonstration (The 'Aha!' Moment)
"Do you have a messy, coffee-stained drawing or a complex multi-page PDF you are currently quoting? I'd love to run it through our system right now to show you. It takes about 10 seconds."
*(Run the drawing. Let them watch the editable UI populate. Show them how they can fix a missed dimension, and then hit 'Download Excel').*

### 3. The Ask & The Offer
"We are currently in a closed Beta and looking for 5 local shops to partner with. If you use this to speed up your quoting process, we will give you the software completely free for the next 3 months. In return, all we ask is that when the system makes a mistake, you tell us so we can improve it."

## Handling Objections
* **"Our drawings are strictly confidential."** * *Response:* "Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade secure endpoints. We don't share your drawings with anyone, and our system is designed to just extract the numbers and delete the temporary files."
* **"It missed the surface finish on this part."**
  * *Response:* "Exactly why we built the edit feature! No system is 100% perfect yet, but correcting one box is much faster than typing the whole sheet from scratch."
